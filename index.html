About Me

I’m an aspiring junior front-end developer hungry to break into the world of web design. I focus on writing accessible HTML, using modern CSS practices and writing clean and readable JavaScript. I am self-driven and independent, learning HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript on my own through various websites, challenges, textbooks, and projects. I’m based in Portland, ME, but I’m happy working remotely as well. I love reading, critical analysis, and, as a former public school Language Arts teacher, have a great respect and dedication to keeping a growth mindset and being a life-long learner in an infinitely interesting world. When I am not coding, I am often found playing and recording music with guitar, mandolin, and/or accordion — unless, of course, I'm off hiking with my dog, Luna. I’d love for you to check out my work.
